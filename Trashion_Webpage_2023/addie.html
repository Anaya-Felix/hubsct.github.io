Under The Sea

 

Designer: Addie

How did you get the Trashion Piece Name? My costume was aiming to be a trash bag UNDER THE SEA

How did you get the idea? I feel very strongly about how healthy our earth is and what it can be so i decided to make my outfit show how much i care and make a statment

What materials did you use? i used black dress to make my top and skirt, new fishing net, and crumpled up peices of paper, feathers ect... for the trash

What have you learned? I have learned how to code a lily pad.
