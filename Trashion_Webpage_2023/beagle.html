Terra

 

Designer: Tani

How did you get the Trashion Piece Name? Terra actually means earth. My piece was based on the climate and earth do I named it Terra.

How did you get the idea? I got the idea because I wanted to make a piece to increase awareness about global warming and the earth.

What materials did you use? I used light green fabric, a sewing machine, and LED lights

How much money do you want for this? $1,000,000,00

What have you learned? I’ve learned how to sew clothing to make it fit together and make a garment that a person could wear. I’ve also learned how to sew on plights and coding lights.
